Mission Statement

To serve as information dissemination tool for both the underprivileged and policy makers on issues that promote national development.

Specific Products & Services

One of our products, a music DVD for Madalitso Choir

G&A International has the expertise to meet the needs of a wide spectrum of clients. This includes production of corporate videos for companies or organisations that would like to let the public know about their activities and products, or training videos for in-house use, as well as reports on diverse topics.G&A International realises that different institutions are investing quite substantial amounts of money in projects aimed at helping improve the living standard of the poor people. But members of the public may not know about this social responsibility until the information is disseminated to them in the form of in-depth feature articles, TV documentaries or panel discussions for both TV and radio.
